SEBI INVESTMENT ADVISERS REGULATIONS - SAMPLE GUIDELINES

Section 1: Risk Profile Classification

1.1 Low Risk Profile:
- Maximum allocation to high-risk assets: 20% of total portfolio
- Maximum single stock concentration: 15% of total portfolio
- Recommended investment horizon: Minimum 24 months
- Suitable for conservative investors seeking capital preservation

1.2 Medium Risk Profile:
- Maximum allocation to high-risk assets: 40% of total portfolio
- Maximum single stock concentration: 25% of total portfolio
- Recommended investment horizon: Minimum 12 months
- Suitable for investors seeking balanced growth

1.3 High Risk Profile:
- Maximum allocation to high-risk assets: 70% of total portfolio
- Maximum single stock concentration: 35% of total portfolio
- Recommended investment horizon: Minimum 6 months
- Suitable for aggressive investors seeking high returns

Section 2: Portfolio Concentration Limits

2.1 Single Stock Concentration:
- No single stock shall exceed 25% of total portfolio value for medium risk profiles
- No single stock shall exceed 15% of total portfolio value for low risk profiles
- No single stock shall exceed 35% of total portfolio value for high risk profiles

2.2 Sector Concentration:
- Maximum allocation to a single sector: 40% of total portfolio
- Diversification across at least 3 different sectors is recommended

Section 3: Risk Management Requirements

3.1 High-Risk Asset Classification:
- Stocks with volatility above 30% are classified as high-risk
- Stocks with risk score above 70 are classified as high-risk
- High-risk assets require additional disclosure and investor consent

3.2 Portfolio Rebalancing:
- Portfolios should be reviewed quarterly
- Rebalancing recommended when allocations drift more than 5% from target

Section 4: Compliance and Disclosure

4.1 Investor Suitability:
- Investment advisers must assess investor risk tolerance
- Portfolio recommendations must align with declared risk profile
- Any deviation from risk profile requires explicit investor consent

4.2 Documentation Requirements:
- All portfolio allocations must be documented
- Risk assessments must be maintained for audit purposes
- Compliance violations must be reported and rectified within 30 days

Section 5: Investment Horizon Guidelines

5.1 Short-term Investments (0-6 months):
- Not recommended for equity portfolios
- Suitable only for high-risk, high-return strategies
- Requires active monitoring

5.2 Medium-term Investments (6-24 months):
- Recommended for medium and high risk profiles
- Allows for market volatility absorption
- Suitable for balanced growth strategies

5.3 Long-term Investments (24+ months):
- Recommended for all risk profiles
- Best suited for low and medium risk profiles
- Allows for compound growth and risk mitigation
